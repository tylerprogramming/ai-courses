ACME CORPORATION — INTERNAL POLICY

1. Data Privacy
The company follows strict data handling rules based on SOC 2 and GDPR guidelines.
Customer information must never be stored in personal drives or emailed externally.
All sensitive data must be encrypted at rest and in transit.

2. Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week.
Exceptions require manager approval.
All remote work must be conducted using a secure VPN.

3. Security Incidents
Any suspected incident must be reported within 24 hours.
The security team will initiate a standard investigation workflow:
identify → isolate → analyze → resolve → document.
